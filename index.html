Furret on Recycling

GAMEPLAY
Collect recyclable items and jump to avoid non-recyclable ones!


HOW TO PLAY (KEYBOARD CONTROLS)
  1. Click to select a difficulty level - EASY, MEDIUM, or HARD.
  2. Press the J key to jump and avoid non-recyclable items.
  3. Click the RESTART button to restart the game at any point.

HOW TO PLAY (VOICE CONTROLS)
  1. Say EASY, MEDIUM, or HARD to select a difficulty level.
  2. Say JUMP to jump and avoid non-recyclable items.
  3. Say RESTART to restart the game at any point.
